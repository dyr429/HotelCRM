DESCRIPTION

Park residence is a 1 bedroom, 1 bathroom unit that can accommodate up to 4 people (sofa included). It is the best location of our themes, being a 5 second walk to the Boston Common Park as well as being central to many restaurants, cafés and shopping centers. The building itself is a beautifully architectured historical site with 24hr security. The room has stunning views of the city, a fully equipped kitchen, TV with Netflix subscription and Xbox. All of this and much more is what you have to look forward to when staying in Park 1.

NEIGHBOURHOODS

Commons Park displays beautiful scenery and welcomes you for tranquil walks under the canopies of its vibrant trees and through it’s wide open grassy flats. Choose any direction and in a short time you’ll find an array of places to see, shop, eat and drink.

Medical centres are closeby, such as, Tufts Hospital (biggest medical center in centre of Boston). Convenient pharmacies and doctors offices are also within a close distance.

Shopping options are in the multitudes with shopping centres such as Macy’s and TJMaxx nearby. Designer shops doors are open with ranges of the newest fashions, including (but not limited to) Hermes, Bottega Venetta, etc

Many international cuisines options are available at a short walking distance from your stay, some of the best Mexican, Middle Eastern, European and Asian restaraunts await your hungry mouths.

Getting Around is a breeze with South Station (main transportation hub for Amtrak and regional buses) only a few minutes walk away and it’s an easy 20 minute drive to and from Boston Logan airport.
